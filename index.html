Powerful Features, Zero Compromise

Everything you need to capture, transcribe, and analyze your meetings - without sending a single byte to the cloud.

Real-Time Transcription

Captures system audio and microphone simultaneously using Whisper AI. Works with Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, or any meeting software.

AI-Powered Analysis

Claude AI generates structured summaries every 60 seconds: key decisions, action items, participants, and next steps - all automatically.

100% Private

All transcription runs locally on your machine using Whisper. No audio is ever uploaded. Your conversations stay yours.

Export & History

Export meeting notes as Markdown or PDF. Browse your full meeting history and search across all past sessions.

Highly Configurable

Choose Whisper model size (tiny to large-v3), select microphone, set language, and control analysis frequency to match your needs.

Works with Everything

Captures audio from any application - Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, browser tabs, or any software playing audio on your system.

Up and Running in 3 Steps

No signup, no browser extension. Just install Claude Desktop, launch the app, and go.

Install Claude Desktop

Download and install Claude Desktop (free). Meeting AI Analyser uses Claude locally to analyze your meetings in real time.

Launch & Join Your Meeting

Double-click MeetingAIAnalyser.exe, then open Teams, Zoom, Meet, or any conferencing tool. The app captures system audio automatically - no bot joins your call.

Get Your Summary

Watch the live transcription and receive an AI-generated structured summary with decisions, action items, and key takeaways. No more manual note-taking.
